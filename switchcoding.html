In this round, participating teams will have to solve C-Programming language-based questions, within a time span of 30 minutes

Round:2 Switch coding contest

In this round the selected team will be given a problem statement to solve.
Only one member of each team will be allowed to code the problem at a given time.
The members will have to switch their places after every 3 minutes.
The teams that codes down the problem first will be declared as the winner.
